About Us

The official IEEE Student Branch of National Institute of Technology Durgapur

IEEE SB NIT Durgapur is a society which aims to promote research related activities among the youth of the campus. The society as a group comprises of keen researchers, spellbinding speakers, creative designers and enthusiastic and hardworking members.

Who are we?

We are a direct handshake to IEEE, an international organization which every year gives innovative brains worldwide a chance to come forward with their ideas for societal change and present it to the world.

What we do?

IEEE Student Branch organizes number of events that help students learn and innovate.The events includes seminars, workshops, Ideathons(which helps young minds help know how they can brainstorm innovative ideas and how to implement them realistically) etc.

Why we do it?

We have always believed in the potential and power of young minds and thus encourages the students to aim for the stars. We plant the seeds of research enthusiasm in the green minds so they can nurture it into a fruitful tree. We believe knowledge is the key to revolution.
